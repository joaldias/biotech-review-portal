Analytical Quality Control

Superior quality is our core commitment. We guarantee the consistency, safety, and purity of every extract batch.

HPLC & GC Validation

NatureXtracts' quality control laboratory is fully equipped with state-of-the-art analytical instrumentation:

  • High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) for compound detection and quantification
  • Gas Chromatography (GC) for volatile compound and essential oil profiling
  • UV/VIS Spectroscopy for rapid concentration assessments
  • Complete reproducibility verification and batch analysis sheets
  • Required regulatory and safety data documentation for global markets

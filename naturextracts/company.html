About NatureXtracts

A green chemistry biorefinery based in Madeira Island, developing sustainable and high-efficiency bioprocesses.

Our Vision & Mission

NatureXtracts is a biorefinery manufacturing through green chemistry. This means that our exploitation of natural resources is based on the development of sustainable processes, high efficiency, and very low environmental impact.

Our mission is to promote the concept of biorefinery by producing and exporting products with high added value, utilizing clean processes with low environmental impact and high technological content.

Our multidisciplinary team—comprising researchers, analytical chemists, and chemical process engineers—works together to provide valuable insights into the development and optimization of natural ingredients.

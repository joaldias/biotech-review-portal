NX Immunity Essentials

June 2021

Co-branding with VitaCholine™ and Albion™, NatureXtracts has just launched NX Immunity Essentials, an essential selection of minerals, vitamins and choline to support and strengthen the immune system.

NX Immunity Essentials contains choline, a new researched product, in combination with vitamin C, vitamin D, vitamin E, zinc and selenium, which have EFSA approved claims on immune system.

NX Immunity Essentials is the first of a range of high quality dietary supplements, specially developed to provide today's consumers with the adequate amount of essential nutrients.

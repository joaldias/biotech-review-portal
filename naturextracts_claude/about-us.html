About Us

A green chemistry biorefinery based on Madeira Island, developing sustainable, high-efficiency natural product processes.

Who We Are

NatureXtracts is a biorefinery manufacturing through green chemistry, meaning that the exploitation of natural resources is based on the development of sustainable processes, high efficiency and very low environmental impact.

NatureXtracts' multidisciplinary team, comprised of individuals from different backgrounds including researchers and process engineers, is highly motivated and works together to provide valuable insight into the development of our natural products.

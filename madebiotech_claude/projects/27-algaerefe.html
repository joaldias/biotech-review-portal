ALGAEREF

Unleashing the commercial potential of Madeira's renewable algae resources through applied biotechnology research.

ALGAEREF Project was partially supported by the EU (FEDER) and the Instituto de Desenvolvimento Empresarial da Região Autónoma da Madeira (IDE-RAM) through the project +Conhecimento Projectos Individuais MADFDR-01-0189-FEDER-0000007. The project was executed between January 1st 2013 and December 31st 2014.

The success and results achieved by the ALGAEREF project are highlighted on the European Commission webpage: Unleashing the commercial potential of Madeira’s renewable resources.

Our Values

The main value of MadeBiotech is the human potential. A company strongly dependent on R&D activities can never forget that the solutions and ideas which it sells will always come from the collaborators. In an almost flat hierarchical structure, merit is recognized regardless of origin, and all are encouraged to freely input their experience, ideas and critique. Therefore, the company's success results from the motivation, the creativity and the well-being of its staff.

With a strong environmental conscience, the company aims for prosperity without losing sight of the social responsibility and the importance of a harmonious relationship with the community in which it is inserted. Although respecting intellectual property and industrial secrets, it is unquestionably necessary to inform the community, as much as possible, of the mission, vision and values of MadeBiotech.

Scientific Partnerships

Collaborating with leading research institutions across Europe to strengthen our R&D pipeline.

The success of any technology-based company with considerable investments in R&D activities is strongly dependent on the quality of its scientific network. MadeBiotech takes this subject very seriously and is constantly working to maintain and, if possible, increase its scientific and technological partnerships with top international institutions, both in the public and private sectors. Over time, this attitude proved to be beneficial for all parties involved since it allows all to take advantage of synergies and complementary knowledge, to help train students and collaborators, to transfer technology and to gain international dimension.

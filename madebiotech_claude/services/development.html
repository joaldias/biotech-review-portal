Technology Development

Turning academic breakthroughs into market-ready value — for institutions without a valorization center of their own.

From Lab to Market

Thousands of good ideas, new techniques, new processes and new products, with incommensurable value, are produced every single day by researchers and graduate students at universities and public institutes. Unfortunately, most of this precious knowledge is not valorized and converted into something with real commercial value, because the necessary development and investment is out of the scope of the researchers and institutions involved.

MadeBiotech comes into play at this stage, helping researchers and institutions without valorization centers get the return from their intellectual portfolio. Only institutions aware of the need for this step will be able to overcome the financial difficulties arising from the lack of public funding.

If you wish to know more about the way we act and how we can help, please contact us.

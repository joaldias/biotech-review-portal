MadeBiotech and NatureXtracts Awarded

June 2018

The 25th edition of the Top 100 Best and Biggest Companies of Madeira took place at the Congress Centre of Madeira in the last November 12th. This event aims to reward companies from various industry sectors that performed best results in 2013. The guest of honor of this edition was the Prime Minister of Portugal, Pedro Passos Coelho.

MadeBiotech and NatureXtracts (processing company of the group) were honored with the following awards:

MadeBiotech-CR & D, S.A.

  • 2nd place for Companies contribution for economy category
  • 1st place Rookie category – Companies contribution for economy
  • 2nd place Rookie category – Dimension
  • 2nd place Rookie category – Financial equilibrium

NatureXtracts, S.A.

  • 2nd place for Dynamism category
  • 1st place Rookie category - Dimension
  • 3rd place Rookie category - Profitability
  • 3rd place Rookie category – Dynamism
